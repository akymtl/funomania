The Spartans are citizens of Sparta, who were trained in the discipline and honor of the warrior society. Subject to military drill from early manhood, the Spartans were one of the most feared military forces in the Greek world. At the height of Sparta's power between the 6th and 4th centuries BC as it was commonly accepted that "one Spartan was worth several men of any other state." According to Thucydides, "it was the common perception at the time that Spartans would never lay down their weapons for any reason, be it hunger, or danger."
